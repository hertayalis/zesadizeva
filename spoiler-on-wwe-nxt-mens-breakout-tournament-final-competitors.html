Spoiler on WWE NXT Mens Breakout Tournament Final Competitors

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 29, 2024

WWE’s next premium live event, New Year’s Evil, will take place in the Performance Center in Orlando on January 2nd. Therefore, the company has ensured they will make the event as memorable as possible. This includes the finals of the NXT Men’s Breakout Tournament and now the final competitors for the tournament have been revealed.

In case you didn’t know, WWE taped the next two weeks’ episodes of NXT. Check here for our complete spoilers for the December 19th episode. You may also click here for the complete spoilers for the December 26th episode.

Both episodes featured the NXT Men’s Breakout tournament in full gear. Lexis King defeated Dion Lennox in a Men’s Breakout Tournament first-round match during the December 19th episode, while Tavion Heights defeated Luca Crusifino.

During the December 26th episode, Riley Osborne defeated Lexis King while Oba Femi defeated Tavion Heights. Obviously, both of them are solid competitors and fans have grown to like both of them.

It was also been confirmed that Riley Osborne will be squaring off against Oba Femi in the finals of the NXT Men’s Breakout tournament. It remains to be seen who will end up winning the tournament.

WWE NXT New Year’s Evil Card:

  • Ilja Dragunov (c) vs. Trick Williams – WWE NXT Title
  • Lyra Valkyria (c) vs. Blair Davenport – WWE NXT Women’s Title
  • Riley Osborne vs Oba Femi – NXT Men’s Breakout Tournament Finals
