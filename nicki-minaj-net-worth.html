Nicki Minaj: Early Life Details

Nicki Minaj was born Onika Tanya Maraj on December 8, 1982, in Saint James, Trinidad and Tobago. She has a brother and sister who are older and a brother and sister who are younger. As a young child, she lived in Saint James with her grandmother. Carol, Minaj’s mother, moved to New York City so she could go to Monroe College.

Nicki was 5 years old when her mom moved the family to Queens, New York. When Minaj was young, Carol worked in the payroll and accounting departments. Minaj’s father had a bad temper and was addicted to alcohol and other drugs. In December 1987, he set fire to the family home.

Minaj tried out for the Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music, Art, and Performing Arts and was accepted. She wanted to be a movie star. After she graduated from high school, she was cast in the Off-Broadway play “In Case You Forget.” To make ends meet, she worked at Red Lobster in the Bronx.

She lost her job because she was rude to customers. Minaj was then fired from “at least 15 jobs” because she was rude.

Nicki Minaj: Personal Life Details

In 2000, Minaj began dating the rapper Safaree Samuels. In 2014, they broke up. Early in 2015, Minaj began dating the rapper Meek Mill. In January 2017, they broke up. Minaj started dating her childhood friend Kenneth “Zoo” Petty in December 2018. In August 2019, she applied for a marriage licence. She said that they would be legally married on October 21, 2019.

Nicholas Telemaque, her cousin, was killed near his home in Brooklyn, New York, in July 2011. She talks about this in her songs “Champion” and “All Things Go.”

In February 2017, burglars broke into Nicki’s house and flipped furniture and threw things all over the place. tearing apart the inside. They got away with $200 000 worth of jewellery and other expensive things.

In November 2017, her brother Jelani was found guilty of sexually assaulting a child and putting a child’s safety in danger. The Nassau County District Attorney’s Office gave him a sentence of 25 years to life on January 27, 2020.

Nicki Minaj: Career Details

Nicki Minaj started making music for the first time in 2004, when she joined the group Full Force and stayed with them for 6 months. In 2007, her first mixtape came out, and in 2008, her second.

Lil Wayne found Minaj in 2009 and offered her a job with his company, Young Money Entertainment. She signed the recording contract, and other up-and-coming Young Money artists used her on several songs that same year.

Minaj’s first album, “Pink Friday,” came out in 2010. It sold more than 375,000 copies in the first week.

This was Nicki’s big break, and it got the media’s attention. Since then, she’s sold tens of millions of singles around the world and become one of the most important hip-hop artists of all time.

Minaj has worked with popular artists like Drake, DJ Khaled, Birdman, Meek Mill, and many more.

Nicki Minaj: Endorsement Income

During her career, Minaj has put her name on a number of products. In November 2010, she worked with MAC Cosmetics to make a lipstick called “Pink 4 Friday.” MAC sold this lipstick for four Fridays in a row to promote her album Pink Friday.

Minaj helped launch the Nokia Lumia 900 in Times Square in April 2012. The next month, Minaj was in TV and online ads for Pepsi’s “LiveForNow” campaign, which used a remix of her song “Moment 4 Life.” In 2012, she and Ricky Martin supported the “Viva Glam” campaign, which raised $270 million for the Mac AIDS Fund.

Minaj was named the new face of Roberto Cavalli’s spring/summer 2015 campaign in December 2014. In 2017, she was in a holiday ad for H&M. Minaj also has a line of perfumes that sell well.
