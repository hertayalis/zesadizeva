Logan Miller biography, stars, films and series

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 5, 2024

Logan Miller is a popular American actor known for the characters he has portrayed in films and television. He has since achieved his breakthrough role as the younger Matthew McConaughey’s character in the film Ghost of Girlfriend’s Past. He has starred in several other films and television series such as Love Simon, I’m in the Band, The Stanford Prison Experiment, A Dog’s Purpose, Growing Up Fisher, and The walking dead.

Miller has warmed his way into the hearts of millions of film viewers around the world, impressing with his acting talent and charisma. He’s one of the rising stars in the industry, and if all goes well, he might one day find his name on Hollywood’s A-list list of actors.

Logan Miller Biography and Celebrity Profile

The Living Dead actor was born on February 18, 1992 in Englewood, Colorado. Although the actor is of American nationality, he is of mixed ancestry: German, English, French and Irish. Logan grew up in multiple places as his parents had to move a lot due to work. He was raised in Albuquerque, New Mexico, Minnesota’s Shakopee neighborhood and Flower Mound, Texas, where his parents currently reside.

Very little information is available about Logan’s Miller family history, parents’ occupations, siblings, or where he went to school. It’s unclear if he finished college or what he studied. Personally, Logan is not yet married, and he has managed to keep his past relationships a secret. Perhaps he prefers to keep his relationship life private since every other aspect of his life is played out in front of cameras and screens.

The Colorado native was previously in a relationship with Elizabeth Gillies in 2011, although it was an open affair and it wasn’t very long before they went their separate ways. After that, Logan dated Caitlyn Taylor Love for a while, but that relationship also ended prematurely. For now, he remains single and focused on building his career.

Logan Miller made his acting debut on the popular television series The Norton Avenue All-Star, playing the character Drew Apple. His role on the series opened the door for him to have a bigger role in the 2009 film Ghosts of Girlfriend’s Past, where he played the young version of Matthew McConaughey’s protagonist. This led to more opportunities to appear in other films like Take Me To The River, The Bling Ring and more. He also played Tripp Campbell in I’m in the Band a Disney comedy series.

The charming actor has been brought further into the spotlight after landing a role on the critically acclaimed series The Walking Dead as the character Benjamin. He also landed starring roles as Kent McFuller in Before I Fall and Collin Croyle in The Scent of Rain and Lightning. Logan Miller also has a side career as a musician. He has also directed, produced and starred in a number of his own short films, some of which he has uploaded to popular streaming platform YouTube.

Movies and TV Shows

Logan Miller has been in the acting industry for almost 10 years during which he has gained a lot of experience by starring in several films and television series which have enhanced his professional resume. The actor has appeared in the following films; Ghosts of Girlfriends Past, Arthur and the Revenge of Maltazard, Deep Powder, +1, The Arm, The Bling Ring, Take Me to the River, Night Moves, Boy Scout’s Guide to the Zombie Apocalypse, and The Stanford Prison Experiment. You might also recognize Miller from his appearances in Would You Rather, The Good Neighbor Before I Fall, The Escape Room, A Dog’s Purpose, The Booty, The Smell of Rain and Lightning, You Can Choose Your Family, and Love, Simon.

The young actor also has a successful acting career on television with series such as Phineas and Ferb, I Hate My Teenage Daughter, Grimm, Ultimate Spiderman, Awake, Dog With a Blog, Growing Up Fisher, Children’s Hospital, and Guardians of the Galaxy.

Logan Miller Net Worth (Income and Earnings)

Logan Miller has had a successful acting career so far and has managed to find fame and fortune along the way. The Love, Simon estimates the actor’s net worth around $3 million, but there’s no information on how much he makes annually from his film and TV appearances.
