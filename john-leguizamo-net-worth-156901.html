Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 30, 2024
John Leguizamo is a Colombian-born comedian and actor who has had a successful career in film and television. He won a Primetime Emmy Award in 1999 for his role in HBO's Freak, and has since starred in a number of films, including Super Mario Bros., A Pyromaniac's Love Story, Collateral Damage, Ride Along, and Kick-Ass 2.
