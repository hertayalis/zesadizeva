Adam Copeland Sets His Sights on Authoring a Childrens Book

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Adam Copeland shocked the world when he made his return at the 2020 Royal Rumble match. Since then, there has been no looking back for the Rated-R Superstar. He was immediately put into a storyline with Randy Orton and then went on to form the Judgment Day.

It didn’t stop there for Adam Copeland as he went on to AEW where he has been competing for the past few months. Copeland is at the tail end of his career and is happy facing the young AEW talent he has never faced. Although he is still an active wrestler, Copeland has a plan for his retirement from the industry.

Adam Copeland recently did an interview with Busted Open Radio where he stated that he wants to write a children’s book and learn to play guitar when he finally hangs up his boots.

“I’d like to write a children’s book. I’ve always wanted to do that, especially now, having kids. I think that would be fun. Maybe part two of the autobiography because there has been almost more since I wrote that first one than took place in the first one. When I retire, retire, I want to properly learn guitar. Just for myself.” 

Adam Copeland will next be in action against Minoru Suzuki on tonight’s episode of AEW Dynamite on quest to recapturing the TNT Championship.
