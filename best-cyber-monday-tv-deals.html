The 26 best Cyber Monday TV deals still available

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, June 10, 2024

Cyber Monday may have been and gone, but there are still plenty of Cyber Monday TV deals sticking around, with big discounts 4K, QLED and OLED TVs. The Cyber Monday deals event was the last big sales event day of the year, but retailers including Best Buy, Amazon, and Walmart haven't ended their deals immediately.

As a deals editor who's been covering Cyber Monday for nearly half a decade, I'm scouring all the offers that are still available to bring you the very best deals on TVs, including our favorites as tested by the TechRadar team.

There are lots of TV deals still going, so I've categorized them by size to help narrow your decision. There's also a section just for OLED TVs because they're so popular. Some highlights include our TV of the Year, the Samsung S90C on sale for $1,299, Samsung's 75-inch 4K smart TV marked down to $579.99, and this TCL 50-inch 4K Roku TV for just $238. 

Keep in mind that some offers will expire at midnight and may not even reappear in the upcoming Christmas sales event.
