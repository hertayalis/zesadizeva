Inside Sarah Paulson And Holland Taylor's Relationship

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Ever since Sarah Paulson declared her love for Holland Taylor in front of millions at the 2016 Emmy Awards, Hollywood's most high-profile same-sex age-gap couple haven't stopped indulging in public displays of affection. The pair always make sure to link arms whenever they hit the red carpet together, and Paulson, in particular, can't stop gushing about her partner on social media.

For example, to celebrate Taylor's 78th birthday in 2021, the Glass star took to Instagram to post a black and white photo of her beau captioned, "All roads lead me to this face, those eyes, that soul. In 2016, just a year into their relationship, she tweeted, "Way over in Manhattan, eagerly awaiting the Emmy red carpet, can't wait for @MsSarahPaulson under ANY circumstances, but, my GOD, HER DRESS!"
