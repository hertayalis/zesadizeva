Lisa Ann Walter Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Lisa Ann Walter:

$400 thousand

Lisa Ann Walter is an American actress. She’s also a comedian and a producer. She had her career beginnings in the mid-1990s, debuting on the sitcom My Wildest Dreams. After this, she starred on another sitcom Life’s Work. During this period, she also had roles in the films Eddie and The Parent Trap. In the 2000s, she became known for her appearances on the big screen, most notably in Bruce Almighty and Shall We Dance?. From 2000 to 2001, she played Cassandra Gilman on the television series Emeril. She next starred as Rachel Glass on the show Breaking News. She recently starred in the 2017 film The Ice Cream Truck.
