Jerry Jeudy

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, June 13, 2024

American Jerry Jeudy is an American footballer. As a wide receiver, he is known for his offensive style. He is currently associated with the Denver Broncos, a professional football team.

What is the Net worth of Jerry Jeudy?

Jerry Jeudy has a net worth of $1 million as of 2021. His base salary with the Denver Broncos is $610k, according to his current deal.

Early Life and Education

Jerry Jeudy was born Jerry Davarus Jeudy in Deerfield Beach, Florida on April 24, 1999. Marie, his mother, is of all-American descent with Haitian ancestors. Later, he was reared by his single mother in Pompano Beach, Florida.

Aaliyah Jeudy was his younger sister. In November 2016, Aaliyah died at the age of seven. Trisomy 18 is a rare chromosomal disorder that she was born with. He attended Deerfield Beach High School in Deerfield Beach, Florida, and graduated with honors. In 2017, he enrolled at the University of Alabama.

Jerry was a wide receiver in high school and was well-known for his abilities. He finished his senior year of high school with 76 receptions for 1054 yards and 15 touchdowns, making him the third-highest ranked wide receiver in the state. He was likewise rated as a five-star prospect at the time.
